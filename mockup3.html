Mockup 3 — “the peak slides earlier” (DWR / OEHHA style)

Monthly Sierra Nevada snowmelt (snm) drawn on a water-year axis (Oct → Sep), exactly like DWR Figure 2. The water year puts the late-winter melt peak near the center of the plot, so the leftward slide of the peak from the warmer scenario is the whole visual story.
